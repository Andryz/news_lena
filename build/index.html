How I got an extra job without doing an extra job
Benito earns extra $2600 each month, and today he will tell us how he did it.
Hi! My name is Benito, and I want to share my holding story with you. First of all, I should notice that crypto has not rang a bell for me. It's funny 'cause it exactly became my extra income.

I've always wanted to give an interview, so sorry for the information you may find unnecessary.

Ooookay. I'm Benito, 26 years old, account manager. Gambling is my way to relax and escape, especially slots and poker. So, last November, I received an email from Fairspin where they told me about Play To Earn and Hold To Earn programs based on the TFS token.

To put it simply, TFS isn't crypto but a digital asset. TFS belongs to Fairspin , and the casino offers two loyalty programs, namely P2E and H2E.

  • Play To Earn means that you get TFS for every bet at Fairspin . Kinda cashback, but they call it rakeback.
  • Hold To Earn means that you can place your tokens on the holding and get a reward after the holding ends.
December 2021. Benito's first steps in tokens

The TFS tokens starter hit I got, thanks to P2E. Then I understood that I could stake my tokens , get more TFS as a reward and send them to my Fairspin account back. They converted into my account currency, and I got kinda free bets.

Then I figured out that I could stake my tokens and withdraw them to my crypto wallet. Not without reason, the Hold To Earn program has its name.

January — March 2022. The holding periods testing and withdrawals

So I decided to buy more TFS 'cause tokens from P2E were not enough to reach my goal. Thus, I top up my Fairspin account by euros, convert them to TFS tokens and begin the holding.

Fairspin offers three periods for holding. When I began my holding practice, it was 8 hours for the speedest H2E option. But not a long time ago, periods have changed a bit. The current ones are:

1. 4 hours
2. 1 day
3. 3 days

I found out that I could withdraw my earned tokens via ETH wallet, so I created the Metamask wallet. I know that there are various crypto wallets, but I heard good reviews about this one.

How Benito's typical holdings look like

My favorite holding period is three days. I understood that I should hold a minimum of €1000 to get some profit. So every three days, I made my holdings: get a reward and hold again.

Notice that the reward for a similar amount of tokens and a similar time may vary. This is because it also depends on the number of holders and casino income.

As for my tactics. First, I constantly withdrew every reward on my crypto wallet and the rest of the tokens I sent for the holding again. When I reimbursed the initial investment, I changed tactics. I began to save half of the revenue (withdrew to a crypto wallet) and left half on the holding for income increase.
